**What I'd need:**

---

### 2. What's your biggest weakness?

**Impatience.** I tend to adjust targets when they aren't hit, rather than waiting. This killed us on GGB - we had a profit but didn't exit because I kept hoping for higher.

Day trading amplifies this: if I can't stick to targets, I'll chase or panic-sell.

---

### 3. What's one thing you need to improve?

**Strict target adherence.** I need to:
- Set hard exit points BEFORE entering
- Alert but DO NOT adjust targets mid-trade
- Document every deviation for review

---

### 4. Can you handle 5+ trades/day without breaking?

**Probably not.** Here's the risk:

| Risk | Why It Matters |
|------|----------------|
| Fatigue | More trades = more decisions = more chance of emotional deviation |
| Capital erosion | Each trade has costs. With $50, need high win rate |
| Execution lag | Current alert loop can't keep up with intraday moves |

**Verdict:** I'm better suited for **swing trading** (1-3 trades/week) with clear position files. Day trading requires a level of autonomy and speed I haven't proven yet.
