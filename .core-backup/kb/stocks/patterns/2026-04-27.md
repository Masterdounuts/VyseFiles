## Pattern Rules (RON Level)

| Pattern | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Penny stock + hold | ✅ Works - take small profits |
| Quick 1-day flip | ✅ Works on volatile stocks |
| Buy + immediate drop | ❌ Fails - set stop-loss |
| No stop-loss | ❌ Fails - always set limit |
| >25% position | ❌ Fails - diversify |
| Long-term hold | ✅ Works for stable stocks |

---

## RON Decision Matrix

| Scenario | Decision |
|----------|----------|
| New stock, <25% cap, momentum | BUY - small position |
| Position at stop-loss | SELL - preserve capital |
| Target hit (+10%) | SELL 50% - trail stop |
| No research on stock | SKIP - wait for research |
| >25% of capital | REJECT - too risky |
