Featured Project

Unreal Engine 5

Skies of Arcadia Fan Remake

My first project in which I try to bring SOA from scratch into the modern age with new textures, models and technology such as Nanite and Lumen in Unreal Engine 5. SOA is a big influence in my pursuit into the gaming industry due to how it affected me when I was young. For a game to convince an adolescent that there is good in humanity has to be one heck of a game.

Vyse's Cutlass

The second weapon I modeled in Blender. Got a better understanding of UV mapping, material editing, and bringing assets into Unreal to Crystallize and Film. Also boosted my knowledge about Unreal Sequencer.

Aika's Boomerang

My first fully modeled weapon that I modeled/textured in Blender then moved over to Unreal to Crystallize and set in the level. I know there are things that can be improved upon but I am very proud of this weapon.

Pirate Isle

The first level introduced world building and level design to me, while also offering an introduction to modeling through Blender. This project also acts as a test bench to incorporate new skills I learn throughout my career.

Pirate Isle Secret Base

The second level showcases the depth and detail I have put into this project. I have learned so much about Unreal Engine's Lumen, Nanite, Fabric Simulation, and gameplay implementation technologies.

Spaceballs The Game

Capstone project with 7-person team (SinDevSeven). Designed gameplay mechanics, created levels, built UI. Rescue mission level with breakable walls, turrets, and escort mechanics.
