## How to Level Up

| Current | Next Level | Requirements |

| 2 → 3 | Competent | Work independently on routine tasks |
| 3 → 4 | Proficient | Handle 3+ edge cases |
| 4 → 5 | Advanced | Self-heal from 3+ errors |
