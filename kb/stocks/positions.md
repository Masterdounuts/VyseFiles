## Trading Rules - SMC METHOD

### The 6 Steps (Complete Guide)
1. **Find Liquidity** — Equal lows, obvious support, prior swing low, clean level everyone sees
2. **Wait for Sweep** — Price dips below liquidity → rejects (liquidity grab)
3. **Wait for CHOCH** — Strong break in structure, invalidates prior trend, clear momentum shift
4. **Wait for FVG** — Price retraces into Fair Value Gap (imbalance) = ENTRY ZONE
5. **Draw Fib** — From liquidity grab low → CHOCH high
6. **TP/SL** — TP at -2, SL below liquidity

### SMC Checklist (Must Have All 6)
- [ ] Liquidity taken (sweep + rejection)
- [ ] Clear CHOCH (break above prior high)
- [ ] Retrace into FVG
- [ ] Fib drawn (liquidity → CHOCH)
- [ ] TP at -2
- [ ] SL below liquidity

**If all 6 line up = SETUP VALID**

### Fib Settings (EXTENSIONS)
- Use: 0, -1, -2 (REMOVE standard retracement levels)
- 0 = Entry point (at CHOCH)
- -1 = First target (1x range expansion)
- -2 = Second target (2x range expansion)

### Position Rules (SMC)
- **Take Profit:** -2 level (2x expansion)
- **Stop Loss:** Just below liquidity grab (with buffer)
- **Max Position:** 25% of capital per stock
- **Max Positions:** 5 stocks at a time

### Why This Works
- Liquidity gets taken → Structure shifts → Price retraces to imbalance → Expansion continues
- No early profit-taking (let expansion complete)
- No emotional exits (math-based targets)
- Consistent R:R

### Volume Checklist
- [ ] Volume trending with trend (confirms)
- [ ] Big candle + low volume = potential reversal
- [ ] Volume spike at key level = institutional activity
- [ ] Divergence (lower highs + higher volume) = reversal likely

### Scanner v4 Criteria
- Near swing low (<45% of range from low)
- Bounced (price above swing low)
- Volume confirmation (>0.5x avg)
- Status: NEAR LIQUIDITY, LOW RANGE, MID RANGE, NEAR HIGH

### Legacy Rules (if no chart)
