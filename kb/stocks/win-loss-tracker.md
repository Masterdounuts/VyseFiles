# Win/Loss Tracker

*Track trades to build confidence for position sizing*

---

## How to Use

1. Log every trade (win or loss)
2. Calculate win ratio after each trade
3. Use ratio to determine position size

---

## Win Ratio Guide

| Win Ratio | Confidence | Position Size |
|-----------|------------|---------------|
| >70% | Very High | 90-100% |
| 60-70% | High | 80-90% |
| 50-60% | Medium | 60-70% |
| <50% | Low | 40-50% |
