## SMC (Smart Money Concepts) — Our Technical Edge

### The Setup (6 Steps)
1. **Find liquidity** — equal lows, swing lows, support
2. **Wait for sweep** — price dips below liquidity, rejects
3. **Wait for CHOCH** — break in structure, momentum shift above prior high
4. **Wait for FVG** — pullback to imbalance zone = entry
5. **Draw Fib** — from liquidity to CHOCH (extensions 0, -1, -2)
6. **Set targets** — TP at -2, SL below liquidity

### Entry Rules
- Must have catalyst BEFORE entry (80% win rate vs 0% without)
- Wait for confirmation, don't predict
- Multiple confluences = higher conviction

## Market Context

### Trend Indicators
- **200-day MA** = major trend line
  - Break below + retest = bearish
  - Hold above = bullish
- **S&P 500 in uptrend** = favor longs
- **Bull market avg +254%**, Bear avg -31%

### Volatility Definitions
| Drop | Term |
|------|------|
| -5% | Pullback (normal) |
| -10% | Healthy correction |
| -15% | Correction |
| -20% | Bear market |
| -30%+ | Crash |

### Range Trading
- Identify support/resistance
- Trade boundaries until break
- Fakeouts common — wait for confirmation

## Volume Analysis

- **Volume up + price up** = strong trend
- **Volume down + price up** = weak trend (reversal likely)
- **Small candle + big volume** = potential reversal
- **Volume spike at key level** = institutional activity

## Position Sizing & Risk

### Rules
- Max 20% per position (diversification)
- -7% stop loss OR below liquidity
- +12% take profit OR -2 Fib extension
- **Never risk more than you can lose**

### Confidence-Based Sizing
- 90-100% confidence = full position
- Less confident = smaller position
- No catalyst = no entry

## Key Learnings Saved

- Catalysts = 80% win rate (no catalyst = 0%)
- 52W low = warning, not always bottom
- "It's low" isn't a thesis — need specific why
- Early stage (0-5% bounce) > late (already bounced)
- Higher timeframe levels = stronger reactions

## Stock Types to AVOID

| Type | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Inverse ETFs** | Move opposite to market, confusing, bleed over time |
| **Leveraged ETFs** | Day-trade only, lose value long-term |
| **ADR** | Currency risk, delisting risk, foreign regulation |
| **Penny Stocks** | High manipulation, low liquidity |
| **Pink Sheet** | Unregulated, fraud risk |
| **Recent IPO** | No track record, lock-up dumps |

**DO NOT INVEST IN INVERSE, LEVERAGED, OR ADR STOCKS.**
