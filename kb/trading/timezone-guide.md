## CRITICAL RULES BY TYPE

### Intraday Trading
- **Entry:** 6:30 AM - 12:30 PM PT
- **Exit:** Same day by 12:30 PM PT HARDCUTOFF
- **No overnight holds**

### Day-to-Day (Swing)
- **Entry:** At market close (1 PM PT)
- **Exit:** Next day close
- **T+1 settlement**

### Mini (Crypto)
- **Entry:** Any time
- **Exit:** Same day preferred (or 1-2 days)
