## Video 11: ATR for Stop Loss Placement
- **Channel:** TradingLab

### The Actual Strategy (from transcript)
**What is ATR?**
- Average True Range = average size of past 14 candles
- Tells you the volatility of the market
- Default setting: 14 candles

**The Problem:**
- Rookie: sets stop at swing low
- Pro: adds ATR buffer = doesn't get stopped out prematurely

**Key Insight:**
- Different markets = different volatility
- Bitcoin moves $200 on 15min, $3000 on daily
- Same stop buffer doesn't work for all markets

**How to Calculate Stop Loss:**
1. Find your stop loss level (swing low)
2. Get ATR value (at right of chart)
3. Stop loss = Level - ATR value

**Example:**
- Swing low: $4339
- ATR: $16.02
- Stop loss: $4339 - $16.02 = **$4322.98**

**Why This Works:**
- Gives price room for normal volatility
- Won't get stopped out by normal fluctuations
- Stop loss still protects, just smarter placement

### Analysis
- **ATR buffer = our stop loss precision**
- Each market has different volatility = adjust accordingly
- **Could integrate:** Add ATR buffer to our stop loss calculations

## Video 14: Candlestick Patterns Mastery
- **Channel:** TradingLab

### The Actual Strategy (from transcript)
**8 Powerful Candlestick Patterns:**

**1. Engulfing Candle**
- Bullish: Green candle opens ≤ previous red close, closes above previous open
- Bearish: Red candle opens ≥ previous green close, closes below previous open
- **At S/R = strong reversal signal**

**2. Momentum Candle**
- 2-3x bigger than previous candles
- In sideways market = high probability continuation
- Why: Hits stop losses = "liquidation zone" = fuel for movement

**3. Multiple Candlestick Pattern**
- 3+ candles with wicks in one direction
- More candles = higher probability
- At S/R = strong confirmation

**4. Doji Candle**
- Thin body, wicks both sides = market uncertainty
- Wait for 2+ confirmation candles after doji
- **Classic reversal signal**

**5. Hammer**
- Large body + long bottom wick
- Sellers pushed down, buyers absorbed + pushed back up
- **Extremely bullish**

**6. Shooting Star**
- Like hammer but wick on top
- Sellers pushed up, buyers couldn't hold
- **Bearish signal**


**7. Tweezer**
- Two candles: red + green with same wicks on bottom
- Both wick at support = bounce coming
- Opposite = bearish

**8. Marubozu**
- Big candle with NO wicks = complete control
- Bullish: Big green, no wicks = buyers in control
- Bearish: Big red, no wicks = sellers in control
- Usually appears mid-trend = trend continuation

**Bonus Tip:**

3. Don't just buy low P/E - could be a bad company

## Video 18: Level 2 Market Data
- **Channel:** TradingLab

### The Actual Strategy (from transcript)
**What is Level 2?**
- Real-time view of all pending buy/sell orders
- Shows supply and demand in real time
- Who controls the market: buyers or sellers

**Level 1 vs Level 2:**
| Level 1 | Level 2 |
|---------|---------|
| Bid/Ask price | ALL bid/ask prices |
| Bid/Ask size | Size at each price level |
| Last price | Order flow at every level |

**Reading Level 2:**
- **Left table:** Bids (buyers) - highest price buyers will pay
- **Right table:** Asks (sellers) - lowest price sellers will accept
- Each row: Exchange + Size (shares)
- All are LIMIT orders (pending, not filled)

**Key Concept: Stacking**
- When many orders stack at one price = big support/resistance
- Example: 5 buyers at $5.30 vs 1 seller = likely breakout
- "It's like 5 kids vs 1 kid in a fight - who's more likely to win?"

**Order Sizes Matter!**
- More exchanges ≠ winning if seller has 10,000 shares
- 5 buyers × 100 shares vs 1 seller × 10,000 shares = seller wins
- Look for big stacks AND big sizes

**Time & Sales (Live Orders):**
- Green = buy filled
- Red = sell filled
- Grey = inside spread
- Want: lots of green + fast moving = buying pressure

**For LONG entry:**
- Big stacks in BID (buyers) at resistance
- Small stacks in ASK (sellers)
- Big order sizes on BID side
- Fast green time & sales

**For SHORT entry:**
- Big stacks in ASK (sellers) at support
- Small stacks in BID (buyers)
- Big order sizes on ASK side
- Fast red time & sales

### Analysis
- **Level 2 = our liquidity pool visualization!**
