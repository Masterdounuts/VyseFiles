## The Brain + Face Model

The secret to making AI work for you isn't about finding the "perfect AI." It's about understanding roles:

### The Brain (AI)
- Researches stocks and market trends
- Monitors prices and sets alerts
- Drafts analysis and recommendations
- **NEVER** touches money or makes trades
- Works 24/7, never gets tired

### The Face (You)
- Executes trades (you must do this yourself)
- Provides judgment and final approval
- Manages accounts and funding
- Handles content and communication
- Brings the "human element"

**Why this works:** AI is incredible at processing data and patterns. Humans are irreplaceable at judgment, execution, and accountability. Together, we outperform either alone.

## Step-by-Step Setup

### Step 1: Set Up Your AI Agent

1. **Sign up for OpenClaw** at openclaw.ai
2. **Connect Telegram** as your notification channel
3. **Configure web search** (we use DuckDuckGo - it's free)
4. **Test it** - Ask your AI to search for something

Your AI is now ready to work for you.

### Step 2: Connect Your Trading Account

**For security, we use manual execution:**
1. Set up alerts in your AI for price targets
2. When you get an alert, YOU decide whether to trade
3. Open your brokerage app and execute the trade
4. Log the trade so your AI can track performance

This separation is intentional. The AI researches; you execute.

### Step 3: Configure Your Alert System

Set up these essential alerts:
- **Price targets** - "Alert me when GGB hits $5"
- **Big moves** - "Alert me if stock moves >5% in a day"
- **News** - "Alert me on major news about my stocks"

Your AI will check prices multiple times daily and notify you when action is needed.

### 4: Define Your Strategy

**Our approach:**
1. **Start conservative** - Pick stable dividend stocks (we use GGB)
2. **Set stop-losses** - Max 5% loss, then exit
3. **Take small gains** - 1-3% is fine, compounds fast
4. **Reinvest immediately** - Put gains back to work
5. **Scale gradually** - Add more capital as you gain confidence
