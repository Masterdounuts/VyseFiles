# Screenshots for Guide

Add your screenshots here to make the guide more visual and sellable.

## How to Add Screenshots

**From your phone:**
- Send photo to me via Telegram
- Say "save for guide"
- I'll grab it and add to this folder

**From your computer:**
- Upload directly to this folder in GitHub
- Name: describe-what-it-is.png (e.g., robinhood-setup.png)

## What to Screenshot

- Your OpenClaw Control UI
- Robinhood account and trades
- Telegram alerts you receive
- Trading confirmations
- Any setup steps
- Morning research results

## Naming Convention

Use descriptive names:
- `robinhood-home-screen.png`
- `telegram-alert-example.png`
- `morning-research-result.png`
- `first-trade-confirmation.png`

## Why This Matters

A guide with real screenshots sells 10x better than text-only. 
Show, don't just tell.
