# Auto-Execute Trading Rules

*Quartermaster's automated trading rules - Updated April 28, 2026*

## Position Rules

**Max position size:** 25% of available capital per trade

**Stop loss:** -15% (exit immediately)

**Take profit:** +20% OR +$70 per position (whichever comes first)
- Intermediate target: $1,400/month ($70/day average)
- ULTIMATE GOAL: THE SKY'S THE LIMIT - No ceiling on monthly earnings

## Execution Rules

- Only execute during market hours (6:30 AM - 5:00 PM PDT)
- Wait for confirmation before executing
- Never execute trades that would exceed 25% position rule
- Alert on opportunities >3% move

## Alert Rules

- Watch for >3% moves in penny stocks
- Check volume + momentum before alerting
- Only alert on real opportunities, not noise
