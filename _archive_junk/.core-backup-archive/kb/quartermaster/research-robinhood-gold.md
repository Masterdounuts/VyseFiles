*Quartermaster Level 6/7 - Multiple Daily Trades Context*

## What is Robinhood Gold?

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Cost** | $5/month |
| **Instant Settlement** | Sells clear immediately (not T+1) |
| **Margin** | Borrow money to trade with more than you have |
| **Interest** | Pay interest on borrowed margin |

---

## For Multiple Daily Trades

### The Math (Our Portfolio: ~$50)

| Scenario | Daily Trades | Monthly Fee Impact |
|----------|--------------|---------------------|
| Current | 1-2/week | $0 |
| With Gold | 5-10/day | $5/mo = 10% of portfolio |

### Does it help us compound faster?

**YES if:**
- We can flip stocks same day (sell → buy → sell → buy)
- Each trade = ~3% gain → 10 trades = 30% potential
- Without Gold: wait 1 day between sell→buy = limits momentum

**NO if:**
- We're not day trading
- The stocks we pick don't move much in 1 day
- $5/month eats profits

---

## The Margin Question

| With Gold | Without |
|-----------|---------|
| Can borrow to double position | Cash only |
| Interest on margin (~5-12%) | No interest |
| **Risk: Margin call** | No margin call |

**Warning:** Margin放大收益也放大损失 - can wipe out portfolio fast.

## Quartermaster's Context

**Our Strategy:**
- Small capital (~$50)
- Penny stocks (volatile, fast moves)
- Quick flips (1-day holds)
- Goal: Compound to $100+

**With Instant Settlement:**
- Sell in morning → Buy different stock afternoon
- Flip same stock twice in one day
- Capture more opportunities
