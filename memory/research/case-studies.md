## Known Examples

### 1. AI-powered Etsy Shops
- **How they make money**: AI generates print-on-demand designs (t-shirts, mugs, stickers)
- **Monthly income**: Varies widely ($0-$5K/month reported)
- **Key learnings**: Non-coders using tools like Canva AI, Midjourney for designs; success depends on trending niches

### 2. AI Content Agencies
- **How they make money**: AI writes blog posts, product descriptions, social media content for clients
- **Monthly income**: $1K-$10K+/month depending on client volume
- **Key learnings**: Non-coders using ChatGPT, Jasper, Copy.ai; upselling retainer contracts

### 3. AI Phone Answering Services
- **How they make money**: AI receptionist services for small businesses (booking appointments, answering FAQs)
- **Monthly income**: $500-$3K/month (per business)
- **Key learnings**: Using platforms like Bland AI, Vapi; non-technical founders scaling via automation

### 4. AI YouTube/TikTok Automation
- **How they make money**: AI generates videos, faceless channels, or thumbnail designs
- **Monthly income**: $0-$2K/month (highly variable)
- **Key learnings**: Non-coders using Runway, Pictory, CapCut AI; algorithm-dependent

### 5. AI Stock Trading Bots
- **How they make money**: Automated trading via algorithmic strategies
- **Monthly income**: Highly variable, -50% to +50%+ reported
- **Key learnings**: Requires capital; risk-heavy; not for beginners

## Self-Sustaining AI Examples

### 6. AutoGPT / AgentGPT Projects
- **How they make money**: Some offer paid tiers for faster execution, API access
- **Monthly income**: Unknown/not publicly disclosed
- **Key learnings**: Open-source → paid premium model; community-driven

### 7. AI SaaS Products (No-code builders)
- **How they make money**: Non-coders using Bubble, Softr, ChatGPT Builder to create AI-powered tools
- **Monthly income**: $0-$50K/month (wide range)
- **Key learnings**: Fastest path to revenue; validation critical

## Brain + Face Model (AI Companions)

### 8. AI Companions / Virtual Friends
- **How they make money**: Subscription models (Replika, Paradot)
- **Monthly income**: Replika reportedly $5M+/month at peak
- **Key learnings**: Subscription + freemium works; emotional connection drives retention

### 9. AI Streamers / VTubers
- **How they make money**: Donations, sponsorships, merch
- **Monthly income**: $0-$10K/month (top performers)
- **Key learnings**: AI brain (LLM) + 2D/3D face; live interaction drives engagement
