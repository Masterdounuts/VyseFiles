### Section 3: What problems do people have with AI trading?

**Search Query:** problems with AI stock trading beginners

**Common Questions/Problems:**
*   Data Quality and Integrity Issues: Sourcing reliable, accurate, and timely data.
*   Algorithmic Complexity and Optimization: Difficulty in developing and optimizing AI algorithms, risk of overfitting.
*   Lack of Transparency (Black Box Problem): Difficulty understanding how AI models make decisions.
*   Over-reliance and Complacency: Neglecting to monitor AI performance and market conditions.
*   Inadequate Risk Management: Lack of stop-loss limits, improper position sizing, leading to substantial losses.
*   Technical Glitches and Latency: Software bugs, connectivity issues, and execution delays.
*   Emotional Amplification: Beginners changing AI parameters hastily based on market fluctuations.
*   High Costs and Accessibility Barriers: High fees, subscription costs, and potential need for coding knowledge.
*   Over-reliance on Historical Data: Past performance not always indicative of future results.
*   Cybersecurity Risks and Fraud: Vulnerability to cyber breaches and fraudulent claims.
*   Lack of Personalized Strategies: AI applying standard strategies that may not align with individual goals.
