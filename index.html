My first project in which I try to bring SOA from scratch by myself into the modern age with new textures, models and technology such as Nanite and Lumen in Unreal Engine 5. Spaceballs The Game

An attempt by myself and fellow students on my team to bring Spaceballs The Game to the public. My level that I brought to the game was Spaceball One which was a rescue mission style level to save the princess.

This level has breakable walls that could be broken with either a turret or the player's Schwartz push ability. Turrets are also able to dispatch enemies which some will drop keys to help the player reach the main goal, rescue the princess. After rescuing the princess the player must escort her back to where the player started.
