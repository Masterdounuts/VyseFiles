## Strategies for $100/day

### 1. Options Trading (Leverage)
- **Credit Spreads:** Generate income from premium
- **Covered Calls:** Own stock, sell calls for income
- **Cash-Secured Puts:** Sell puts to collect premium
- **Math:** With $1,000 account, 10% return = $100/day potential with leverage

### 2. Momentum Day Trading
- Trade mid-cap/large-cap stocks (not penny stocks)
- Focus on: breakout, momentum, news-based plays
- Risk: Higher - requires skill and time

### 3. Dividend Income
- **REITs** - Higher yields (5-8%)
- **Dividend ETFs** - Stable income
- **Covered Call ETFs** - JEPI, JEPQ, etc. (8-12% yield)
- **Math:** $30,000/month = $360,000/year
  - At 6% yield need $6M portfolio
  - At 10% yield need $3.6M portfolio

### 4. Swing Trading
- Hold positions 2-5 days
- Target 3-5% gains per trade
- 2-3 good trades/week = $100+
