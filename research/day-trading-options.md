## Best Options Strategies for Small Capital

### 1. Credit Spreads (Best for Small Accounts)
- Sell OTM spread, buy for protection
- Collect premium, limited risk
- Can start with $100-500
- **Target:** Turn $100 → $500 (5x)

### 2. Cash-Secured Puts
- Sell puts on stocks you want to own
- Collect premium
- If assigned, you get the stock at discount

### 3. The Wheel Strategy
- Sell puts → get assigned → sell covered calls → repeat
- Generates consistent income

### 4. Weekly Options
- Sell weeklies (5-7 DTE)
- Collect time decay faster
- Requires more active management
