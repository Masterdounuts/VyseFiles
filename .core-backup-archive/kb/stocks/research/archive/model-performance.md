# Model Performance Log

Tracking which models work best for different tasks.

---

## openrouter/minimax/minimax-m2.5
- **Default model**
- Good for: General conversation, summaries, quick tasks
- Speed: Fast
- Cost: Free

## openrouter/openai/gpt-oss-120b
- **Fallback model**
- Better for: Complex reasoning, long documents
- Used for: Heartbeat, heavy analysis
- Speed: Slower
- Cost: Free

## google/gemini-2.5-flash
- **Image analysis**
- Good for: Analyzing screenshots, documents
- Speed: Fast
- Cost: Free
